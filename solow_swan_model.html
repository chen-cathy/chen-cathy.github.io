Solow-Swan Model: Capital Accumulation & Convergence

January 2026 | Author: Economic Research Team

Executive Summary

This report consolidates the analysis of the Solow-Swan growth model applied to selected economies (Singapore, Taiwan, Czech Republic, South Korea). Part I examines the capital accumulation phase and GDP growth trends compared to the theoretical exponential model. Part II analyzes the convergence hypothesis, testing both \(\beta\)-convergence (catch-up effect) and \(\sigma\)-convergence (reduction in dispersion) over the period 2000‚Äì2024.
