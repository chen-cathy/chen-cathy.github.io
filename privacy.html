Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, specifically through our contact form. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Data Collection

When you use the contact form on our website, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To address you appropriately in our response
  • Email address: To respond to your inquiry
  • Message content: To understand and respond to your inquiry or feedback

This information is collected only when you voluntarily submit it through our contact form. We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect additional personal information.

Purpose of Collection

The personal information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries, questions, or feedback
  • To provide you with information you have requested
  • To maintain a record of our communication for reference purposes

We will only use your data for the purposes stated above and will not use it for marketing or any other purpose without your explicit consent.

Data Protection and Third-Party Sharing

We take the security and confidentiality of your personal information seriously:

  • No third-party sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes
  • Service providers: We use Formspree as our form processing service. Your submitted data passes through their secure servers to reach our email inbox. Formspree is bound by their own privacy policy and security measures
  • Data security: Your information is transmitted securely and stored in our email system with industry-standard security measures
  • Data retention: We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of any inaccurate personal information
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your personal information from our records
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your personal information
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact form on our website. In your message, clearly state which right you wish to exercise and provide sufficient details to identify your data. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
